Prime Minister Nabbanja drums up support for Parish Development Model program

News We Are Uganda 16 April 2022

Hon Robbina Nabbanja

The Prime Minister Rt Hon Robbina Nabbanja has asked local leaders at all levels to spearhead the massage about Parish Development Model programme in their areas of jurisdiction. Hon Nabbanja made the remarks on Friday while speaking at a one day sensitisation workshop for Parish Development Model at Kakumiro District Headquarters.

The workshop was organized by Hon Nabbanja who is also the Woman Member of Parliament for Kakumiro District in an effort to help the district leaders understand more on how the Parish Development Model program will operate. The workshop was addressed by the Minister for Local Government Raphael Magyezi, State Minister for Transport Fred Byamukama, Deputy Coordinator Operation Wealth Creation Maj Gen Sam Kavuma and the the National PDM Coordinator Denis Ssozi Galabuzi.

The workshop was attended by Kakumiro District Area Members of Paiament, District political and technical leaders from District and Subcounty levels leaders. The Prime Minister tasked participants to spread the Parish Development Model gospel to their respective areas of jurisdiction after the training in order for the beneficiaries to understand and appreciate it.
